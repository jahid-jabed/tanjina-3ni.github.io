Codeforces for newbies: Learn with Fun!

Codeforces | 28 May, 2021

How to sort out the easiest problems in Codeforces?

As a newbie in codeforces, we should start with the easiest problem. But how could we find the easiest problem? Here is the easiest way to find the easiest problem.


  • Step 1: Log on to your Codeforces account.

  • Step 2: Click on PROBLEMSET to discover all problems at once as shown in following screenshot.


  • Step 3: Click on Tick-Mark as shown in following screenshot to sort all problem as most solved one first.


  • Step 4: Hurrah! All the easiest problems are shown first. Now enjoy problem solving!



How to find out specific types of problems in Codeforces?

Sometimes we want to solve specific types of problems such as - mathematical, binary search related, or brute force problems. We can find out our domain of interest by doing following steps -


  • Step 1: Log on to your Codeforces account.

  • Step 2: Click on PROBLEMSET to discover all problems at once as shown in following screenshot.


  • Step 3: Click on the brute force tag to show all problems can solve using brute force principle.


  • Step 4: Now all problems related to the brute force are shown.
