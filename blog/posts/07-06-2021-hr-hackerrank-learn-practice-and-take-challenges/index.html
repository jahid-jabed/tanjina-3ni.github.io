HackerRank: What and Why?


HackerRank is an online platform where developers all over the world can solve problems of various domains of Computer Science. Developers can practice their problem solving skills. They can also learn programming languages such as, C, C++, Python, Ruby etc. Not only that, they can practice basic algorithms and data structure with fun. We all know that data structures and algorithms are the heart of programming. One can practice Artificial Intelligence (AI), database, Linux shell etc. If you want to learn with fun, then this platform is going to be very helpful to you.


5 Interesting Facts about HackerRank:


  1. By solving each problem you will get specific points!
  2. After gaining a specific number of points you will earn a star for your badge. It’s like a game, isn’t it?
  3. You can discuss this with other developers from all over the world. So, there is an opportunity to prepare yourself for the international platform.
  4. If you need a tutorial on the problem then you will get it here.
  5. You can separate problems into simple, medium, and hard. Not only that, you can practice your skill-wise for example, basic, intermediate, advance, problem-solving. You can also divide them into subdomains.
